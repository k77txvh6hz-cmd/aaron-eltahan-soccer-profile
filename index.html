About Aaron

I'm Aaron Eltahan, a hardworking, disciplined, and coachable player who focuses on steady development and contributing to the team in meaningful ways. I currently play for FC Westlake on the 2010 ECNL RL STXCL team, primarily as an attacking midfielder and winger. Before joining Westlake, I spent six years at Lonestar (2019–2024), playing mainly as a winger and striker while competing in league play and tournaments.

I have trained continuously for years, beginning with Avila Soccer and now with 1A Soccer Sports Club for ongoing individual technical development. Strength and conditioning sessions several times weekly support my athletic performance. Coaches describe me as extremely coachable with strong movement, vision, positioning, and reliability in possession.

I maintain a 3.960 cumulative GPA at Early College High School, where I will graduate with both a high school diploma and an ACC associate degree, with ACC courses counting as advanced placement. When I'm not in school or at practice, I work as a certified lifeguard, lift weights with friends, ride my dirt bike, and enjoy FIFA and other online games.

My goal is to compete at the college level while pursuing kinesiology, sports medicine, or another health-science major.
